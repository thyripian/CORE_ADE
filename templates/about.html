Part of the CORE enterprise.

The Centralized Operational Reporting Engine (CORE) was originally developed to facilitate databasing and querying of Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint presentations, and Adobe PDFs that were stored on shared drives, providing an efficient way to manage and search through large collections of files without a practical solution in place.

This lightweight version of the same application has been developed specifically for use in offline and austere environments. It was built as a BYOD (Bring Your Own Database) solution, allowing any prebuilt SQLite database to be queried in real time, driving operational awareness and fulfilling intelligence needs.
